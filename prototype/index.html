History

The University of Arkansas at Little Rock (UA Little Rock) was founded in 1927 as Little Rock Junior College. Initially created by the Little Rock School District, it became a private four-year university in 1957 under the name Little Rock University. In 1969, the institution joined the University of Arkansas System and adopted its current name. Today, the campus spans 250 acres with more than 56 academic and research buildings.

Academics

UA Little Rock offers over 100 undergraduate and 60 graduate programs, including graduate certificates, master’s, and doctoral degrees. Students take courses both on campus and online across the following academic divisions:

  • College of Business, Health, and Human Services
  • College of Humanities, Arts, Social Sciences, and Education
  • Donaghey College of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics
  • William H. Bowen School of Law

Student Life

Life at UA Little Rock is enriched through student organizations, cultural events, and leadership opportunities. The university supports intramural sports, diversity programs, student government, peer mentoring, and a student-run newspaper. The campus’s location in Little Rock gives students access to internships, cultural venues, and career opportunities.

Campus Living

UA Little Rock offers four residence halls and the University Village Apartment Complex, providing students with both traditional dorms and apartment-style living. Specialized learning communities in areas such as STEM, business innovation, nursing, and arts and culture help students connect academically and socially.

Athletics

Known as the Little Rock Trojans, UA Little Rock competes in 14 NCAA Division I sports. While the school does not sponsor football, it offers a wide variety of programs including basketball, baseball, volleyball, golf, soccer, cross country, track and field, swimming and diving, and wrestling.

The Trojans primarily compete in the Sun Belt Conference but will transition to the Ohio Valley Conference beginning in the 2022–23 season.
